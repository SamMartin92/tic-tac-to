Tic Tac To 15

How to play:

  • Play vs another player or the computer
  • The goal of the game is to make a the contents of a full row, column or diagonal add to 15
  • The first player selects a square to add the number 1. The next player selects another to add the number 2.
  • The first player then adds 3 to a square.
  • The game continues in this pattern until three numbers in a row, column or diagonal sum up to 15.
  • The winner is the first player to complete this goal.
  • If all squares are filled with no combinations adding up to 15, a tie is declared.
